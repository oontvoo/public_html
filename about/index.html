Welcome!!!
I am a student from UMass Boston, majoring in Computer Science. I very much enjoy what I am doing (ie., coding, solving puzzles. What else do we do?), though I am certainly not able to dream in code, nor do I possess unbelievably extraodinary abilities or skills. I find my ordinary ability to reasonably retain and apply the things that I have learned and bit of passion for the things that I am doing quite helpful, if not sufficient, in tackling most problems that I have faced.
